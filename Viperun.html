About the Game

VipeRun is a fast-paced endless runner for iOS inspired by the classic snake game. Slither through vibrant levels, dodge obstacles, collect coins, and see how far you can go. Customize your snake with a selfie avatar and compete against players worldwide on the Game Center leaderboards.

Whether you have one minute or one hour, VipeRun delivers addictive, pick-up-and-play fun that keeps you coming back for more. Free to download and play.

Features

📸
Personalized Avatar

Take a selfie to set your unique in-game player avatar.

🏃
Endless Runner

Dodge obstacles and survive as long as possible across endless levels.

🪙
Coins & Power-Ups

Collect coins to unlock power-ups that give you an edge in the run.

🏆
Leaderboards

Compete globally via Apple Game Center leaderboards and achievements.

🆓
Free to Play

Download and play for free, supported by ads.
