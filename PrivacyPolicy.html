Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

Chandra Shankar Shrestha ("we," "us," or "our") built the VipeRun app as a free app. This page informs visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if anyone decides to use our Service. By using VipeRun you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Camera and Photos (Avatar Feature)

VipeRun requests camera access solely to let you take a selfie for your in-game player avatar. All image processing and storage happen strictly on your device. We never transmit, upload, or store your photos or facial data on any external server. Deleting the app permanently removes this data.

Game Progress and Local Storage

Your game progress, high scores, coins, and unlocked items are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage frameworks. We do not have access to this data.

2. Information Collected Automatically (Advertising)

To support the ongoing development of VipeRun, we use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. When you use the app, AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Your device's IP address
  • Device attributes (make, model, operating system)
  • Mobile advertising identifiers (Apple IDFA)
  • App usage data and interactions with advertisements

App Tracking Transparency (iOS 14+)

In compliance with Apple's guidelines, we prompt you with the App Tracking Transparency framework before tracking your activity across other companies' apps or websites for targeted advertising. You can change this preference at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob — Google Privacy Policy · How Google uses data
  • Apple Game Center — Your Game Center username and leaderboard scores are managed by Apple. Apple Privacy Policy

4. Security

Because VipeRun operates primarily as an offline game, the vast majority of your data never leaves your device. For data that does leave your device (ad-related), we rely on Google's industry-standard security practices. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Links to Other Sites

Advertisements within the app may link to external websites. These sites are not operated by us, and we have no control over their content or privacy practices. We strongly advise reviewing their Privacy Policies before providing any personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

This Service does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take the necessary steps to remove it. Advertisements served by Google AdMob are configured to comply with COPPA where applicable.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting to this page. We advise you to review this page periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
